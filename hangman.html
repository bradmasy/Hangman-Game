How To Play Hangman:


1: Choose A Letter
Choose a letter that you think might be in the chosen word. When a letter is chosen it is either in the sequence of characters or not. If the letter is in the sequence, the chosen letter will pop up in the field where it occurs in the word. If not the hangman image will animate the first of 6 sequences before you have lost.

2: If your stuck, click the hint button to generate a new hint.
Your score will be effected for each hint generated! So be careful!.

3: If you would like to play a new game and start over with a different word, click the new game button!
There are many words to choose from so enjoy the ride!

4: Have fun and try to figure the game out before clicking on hints!
Enjoy testing your knowledge!

About Hangman...

Welcome to Hangman!
Hangman at the core is a guessing game
designed to have the user guess the given word
In this game you have 6 attempts to guess the chosen
word before you have lost.
With each failed attempt your character will continue to
draw itself until fully completed
Good Luck!
